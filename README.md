## English Version

**LLM‑SLWI (Large Language Models for Second-Language Writing Interventions)**

The LLM‑SLWI project develops a rigorous, multi-level empirical framework to evaluate large language models as interventions in second-language writing. By integrating fine-grained process data (e.g. keystroke and interaction logs), writing product quality, learner strategy use, and equity-sensitive outcome metrics, the framework links how learners write, what they produce, how they adapt their strategies, and who benefits under which conditions. This design enables the identification of causal effects and heterogeneous impacts of LLM-mediated feedback across proficiency levels, task types, and sociolinguistic groups, as well as the systematic characterization of failure modes in authentic instructional settings. Ultimately, LLM‑SLWI seeks to specify evidence-based conditions for the safe, pedagogically robust, and scalable deployment of large language models as supports for second-language writing development.


## 中文版本

**LLM‑SLWI（大型语言模型在第二语言写作干预中的应用）**

LLM‑SLWI 项目旨在构建一个严格的多层次实证框架，用于系统评估大型语言模型在第二语言写作干预中的效果。该框架综合分析写作过程数据（如键击与交互记录）、写作产出质量、学习者策略使用情况以及聚焦公平性的结果指标，将 "如何写" "写得如何" "如何调适策略" 与 "在什么人群中产生何种影响" 有机联结。由此，项目不仅能够识别 LLM 介入写作反馈的因果效应及其在不同水平、不同任务类型和不同社会语言群体中的异质影响，还能在真实教学情境下系统刻画其失效模式与潜在风险。最终，LLM‑SLWI 旨在给出基于证据的应用边界与前提条件，为大型语言模型在第二语言写作中的安全、教学有效且可规模化应用提供坚实的研究基础。
